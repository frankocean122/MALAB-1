About Our Laboratory

MALAB (AI and Bioengineering Lab) is dedicated to advancing the intersection of artificial intelligence and bioengineering, developing innovative solutions at the frontier of these rapidly evolving fields.

Our multidisciplinary team combines expertise in molecular biology, computer science, and bioinformatics to develop novel approaches for designing and engineering biological systems with unprecedented precision and efficiency.

Research Highlights

Our laboratory focuses on several key research areas at the intersection of synthetic biology and artificial intelligence 我们的实验室专注于合成生物学与人工智能交叉领域的几个关键研究方向

AI for Protein Design

Developing machine learning algorithms to predict and design protein structures with specific functions for biotechnology applications.

蛋白质设计的人工智能方法

Synthetic Gene Circuits

Engineering complex genetic circuits that can sense, process information, and respond to environmental stimuli in living cells.
